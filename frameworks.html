EMeRGE

Emerging technologies Management and Risk evaluation on distribution Grids Evolution

2020, https://github.com/NREL/EMeRGE

Source: National Renewable Energy Laboratory (NREL)

Read: Approx. 10 minutes

EMeRGE is an opensource software framework developed using Python at National Renewable Energy Laboratory (NREL) in 2020. This tool allows user to develop distribution feeder model using GIS data, develop customized solar deployment scenarios, compute and compare risk metrics at both system and asset level and visualize analysis results in an easy to use interactive dashboard.
